LifeLab Foundation is a non-profit that inspires and nurtures the scientists of tomorrow. It is founded and led by Dr. Archana Sharma (Principal Scientist, CERN). Its goal is to educate students beyond the school curriculum to explore modern scientific research and its applications in the real world. Through engagement programs for students and teachers, it exposes young people to the joy of scientific inquiry to further their interest in STEM subjects and associated careers to make a difference.
